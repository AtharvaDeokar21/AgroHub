About AgroHub

AgroHub is a comprehensive digital solution for farmers, offering powerful tools to manage their farm activities, optimize crop yield, and keep track of sales and resources. Our platform empowers farmers to make data-driven decisions, automate workflows, and achieve greater productivity with ease.

Key Features

Comprehensive Crop Management

Track crop cycles, planting dates, and harvest yields. Monitor each crop’s growth to optimize production and reduce waste.

Efficient Sales Tracking

Record your sales data with ease, keep track of total earnings, and access detailed analytics to improve profitability.

Resource Allocation

Manage fertilizers and pesticides, assign them to specific crops, and monitor usage for cost-effective farming.

Interactive Dashboard

Get an overview of all your farming activities in one place. Access reports, charts, and forecasts at a glance.
